Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top navigation and fill out the registration form with your details.

How do I list an item for sale?

After logging in, click on "Sell" in the navigation and fill out the product details form with images of your item.

How do payments work?

Buyers and sellers arrange payment directly. We recommend using secure payment methods like UPI or cash on delivery.

Is there a fee for using Student Mart?

No, Student Mart is completely free for students to buy and sell items within their campus.

Safety Tips

Meet in Public Places

Always meet buyers/sellers in well-lit, public areas on campus. Avoid meeting in private locations.

Bring a Friend

Consider bringing a friend with you when meeting to complete a transaction.

Inspect Items Carefully

Thoroughly inspect any item before purchasing to ensure it matches the description.

Use Secure Payment Methods

Prefer UPI payments or cash on delivery. Avoid advance payments unless you trust the seller.
